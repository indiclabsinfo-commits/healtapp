01 — How It Works

The MindCare Flow

From onboarding to ongoing wellness — here's how your organization uses MindCare.

01
🏫

Organization Onboards

Your school or company gets a unique Organization Code. Admin sets up departments/classes, adds counsellors, and loads credits.

02
👤

Users Join via Code

Students or employees register using the org code. They get role-based access — student, teacher, HR, or counsellor.

03
📋

Assessments & Assignments

Admin assigns wellness assessments. Users take quizzes, get scored, and results flow into analytics. Teachers can flag students who need help.

04
🎤

Book Consultations

Users browse counsellors, pick a time slot, and book using org-provided credits. In-house or external counsellors — all managed centrally.

05
🌱

Self-Care Tools

Guided breathing exercises with animated visuals. Theory sessions with progress tracking. Mood logging for personal wellness trends.

06
📈

Analytics & Insights

Admin sees organization-wide wellness metrics. Teachers see class reports. HR gets department wellness dashboards. Real data, real decisions.
